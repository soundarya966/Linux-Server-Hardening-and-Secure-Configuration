## Questions

### What is server hardening?
Server hardening is the process of securing a system by reducing vulnerabilities, disabling unnecessary services, and enforcing strong configurations.

### Why disable root login?
Disabling root login prevents attackers from directly accessing the most privileged account.

### What is least privilege?
Least privilege means giving users only the permissions necessary to perform their tasks.

### Purpose of firewall?
A firewall filters network traffic and blocks unauthorized access.

### Risks of unused services?
Unused services may contain vulnerabilities and increase the attack surface.
